Ivan Smirnov

I am a final-semester B.Sc. student in Artificial Intelligence at Deggendorf Institute of Technology. My focus is on machine learning, reinforcement learning, and robotics. I am also a memeber of UnitedAI club.

Currently I am a Working Student at BMW Group in BMW Research and Technology House, Garching b. Munich, where I am working on end-to-end decision-making in autonomous driving. I have experience working with research companies like Fraunhofer IGCV, where I contributed to developing machine learning models and MLOps pipelines.

I have also held a position as an Assistant IT Manager at Refresh-K LLC, where I developed enterprise solutions to streamline operations and enhance efficiency. I aim to further my research in AI, particularly in robotics, after completing my degree. I am actively exploring opportunities for PhD programs, with a focus on reinforcement learning and application to adaptable robotic systems.
